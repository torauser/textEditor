SANO SCRIPTUM

Here you may easily edit your texts and find out more about some if its properties.

The editor allows you to:

Remove specific words

Copy your text into the left field. Type the words you want to be removed in the field immediately below the text. Click 'Edit' button. You're done!

Replace some words.

First, you need to type a word or words you want to be replaced. Then type a word that should replace previously chosen words into the following field. Click the button and that's it!

Find out the total amount of words before and afer editing.

There is almost nothing you have to do. Just paste your text and you will see the details.

Find out the amount of sentences.

Umm, just paste your text.

See how many times a word was used in your text.

Type the word you are interested in and there you have it. Providing you have pasted your text beforehand.
